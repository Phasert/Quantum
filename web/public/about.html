Policies

Reservations and Payment Cancellations and Changes Condition of Items

A non-refundable deposit of 50% is required to reserve items. Full payment is due at least 7 days before the rental date. Payment can be made via credit card, debit card, or bank transfer.

Cancellations made more than 14 days before the rental date will receive a refund minus the deposit. Cancellations made less than 14 days before the rental date will forfeit the full payment. Changes to the order must be made at least 7 days in advance and are subject to availability.

All items are rented out clean and sanitized. Customers are responsible for returning items in the same condition. A cleaning fee may apply if items are returned excessively dirty.

Damage and Loss Customer Responsibilities Use of Items

Customers are responsible for any damage or loss of items during the rental period. A security deposit of 50% of the rental fee is required and will be refunded upon return of undamaged items. Charges for damaged or missing items will be deducted from the security deposit. If the deposit is insufficient, the customer will be billed for the remaining amount.

Customers must inspect items upon receipt and report any issues within 24 hours. Items must be used and stored in a safe and secure manner. Customers must ensure that items are returned in the same containers and packaging as delivered.

Items are for event use only and should not be used for any other purpose. Customers must follow all care instructions provided for cutlery, glassware, and linen.
